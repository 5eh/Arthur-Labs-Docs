## What is FRNT?

FRNT (pronounced "front") is Wyoming's official stablecoin, backed 1:1 by US dollars held in trust by the state. Unlike USDC or USDT, which are issued by private companies, FRNT carries the full faith and regulatory oversight of a US state government.

### Key Features

- **1:1 USD Parity** â€” Each token represents exactly one US dollar held in trust
- **State-Backed Trust** â€” Full regulatory compliance and oversight from the Wyoming Stable Token Commission
- **Blockchain Native** â€” Built on Solana for fast, low-cost transactions
- **Redemptable** â€” Holders can redeem FRNT for physical USD through authorized channels

## How Wyoming Created the First State Stablecoin

Wyoming has been building toward this moment for years. Here's the timeline that led to FRNT:

### 2021: The DAO Framework

Wyoming became the first US state to legally recognize Decentralized Autonomous Organizations (DAOs) as legal entities. This created the foundation for blockchain-native governance.

### 2023-2024: Stable Token Commission

The Wyoming Legislature established the Stable Token Commission, tasked with exploring a state-issued digital currency. The commission studied existing stablecoins, regulatory frameworks, and technological requirements.

### 2025: FRNT Launches

After extensive pilot programs with companies like Fundora, FRNT went live on Solana in late 2025, making Wyoming the first US state to issue its own stablecoin.

### 2026: Ecosystem Expansion

The FRNT ecosystem is growing rapidly. DeFi platforms, payment processors, and lending protocols are integrating the token, creating the first state-backed stablecoin economy.
