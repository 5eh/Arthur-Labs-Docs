## Information Collection

Our marketplace platforms collect the following types of information:

- **Blockchain Data**: Public blockchain information, including wallet addresses and transaction records
- **Profile Information**: Optional details you provide in your marketplace profile
- **Transaction Details**: Information necessary to complete marketplace transactions
- **Communication Content**: Messages exchanged through platform messaging systems
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our platforms
- **Device Information**: Technical details about the devices used to access our platforms

## Information Use

We use collected information for the following purposes:

- **Facilitating Transactions**: Enabling secure buying and selling on the platform
- **Platform Improvement**: Enhancing and optimizing marketplace functionality
- **Security Measures**: Protecting against fraud and unauthorized access
- **Dispute Resolution**: Resolving issues between marketplace participants
- **Communication**: Sending important updates and information about the platform
- **Analytics**: Understanding platform usage patterns and user preferences

## Information Protection

Arthur Labs employs the following measures to protect your information:

- **Blockchain Technology**: Utilizing decentralized systems with inherent security features
- **Encryption**: Implementing modern encryption standards for sensitive data
- **Access Controls**: Limiting information access to authorized personnel
- **Regular Security Reviews**: Conducting ongoing assessments of our security practices
- **Smart Contract Audits**: Regular verification of platform smart contract security
- **Data Minimization**: Collecting only information necessary for platform functions

## User Rights

As a user of our platform, you have the following rights regarding your information:

- **Access**: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
- **Correction**: You can request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: You can request deletion of certain information where appropriate
- **Objection**: You can object to certain types of processing of your information
- **Data Portability**: You can request transfer of your information in certain circumstances
- **Withdrawal of Consent**: You can withdraw consent for optional data processing

Please note that some information is essential for platform functionality and cannot be deleted while maintaining your account.

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our data protection team.
