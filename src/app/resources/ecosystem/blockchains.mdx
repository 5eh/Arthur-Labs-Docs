### Integration Details

Arthur Labs' ROSE system integrates with Ethereum through:

- **Solidity Smart Contracts**: Our commerce, service, and delivery contracts are primarily written in Solidity for deployment on Ethereum.
- **Web3.js and Ethers.js**: We use these libraries to interact with the Ethereum blockchain from our applications.
- **MetaMask Integration**: Users can connect their MetaMask wallets to interact with our marketplaces.

### Protocol Integrations

On Ethereum, we integrate with several specialized protocols:

- **XYO**: On-chain GPS tracking for delivery verification
- **Waku**: On-chain messaging for secure communications
- **Kleros**: On-chain dispute resolution for transaction conflicts

Tezos integration in ROSE provides additional security benefits:

- **camelLIGO and SmartPy Contracts**: We develop smart contracts in camelLIGO and SmartPy for Tezos deployment.
