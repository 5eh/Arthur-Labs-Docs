---

## Category Rules

1. **One primary category per post**
2. **Maximum 3 sub-categories**
3. **Use consistent URL prefixes**
4. **Include category in frontmatter**

---

## Frontmatter Schema

```yaml
category: Tax & Compliance
subcategories:
  - Federal Tax
  - Compliance
tags:
  - crypto-tax
  - 1099-da
  - irs
```
