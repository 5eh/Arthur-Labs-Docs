## SEO Strategy

### Keyword Strategy

**Primary Keywords (1 per post):**
- High search volume
- Commercial intent
- Competitive but achievable

**Secondary Keywords (2-3 per post):**
- Long-tail variations
- Informational intent
- Lower competition

**LSI Keywords (scattered):**

- Synonyms
